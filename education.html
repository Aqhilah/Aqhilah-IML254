MY EDUCATION JOURNEY

Education is the foundation for a brighter future, empowering individuals with knowledge and skills to achieve their dreams.


Taski Abim SK Haji Hussin Dol SMK Guar Chempedak UiTM Kedah
I began my early education at Taski Abim when I was 5 to 6 years old, where I developed the foundation of my learning and social skills. I continued my primary education at SK Haji Hussin Dol from the ages of 7 to 12, where I gained a deeper understanding of various subjects and participated in extracurricular activities. For my secondary education, I studied at SMK Guar Chempedak from 13 to 17 years old, a period that helped shape my academic and personal growth through challenging coursework and enriching school experiences. After completing secondary school, I enrolled in a Pre-Diploma program at UiTM Kedah at the age of 18, which prepared me for higher education.Now, at 19 and continuing into 20 years old, I am pursuing a Diploma program, further expanding my knowledge and skills to achieve my future career aspirations.
